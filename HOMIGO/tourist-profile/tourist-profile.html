About Emma

Hello fellow travelers! I'm Emma, a passionate explorer from London who believes that the best way to truly understand a place is through the eyes of locals. Over the past 5 years, I've been traveling the world, seeking authentic experiences that go beyond typical tourist attractions.

My journey with Homeigo started when I was looking for genuine cultural experiences in Southeast Asia. Since then, I've stayed with incredible hosts, learned traditional cooking techniques, participated in local festivals, and made lifelong friendships across 18 countries.

I love adventure sports, trying new cuisines, learning languages, and capturing moments through photography. My goal is to inspire others to travel mindfully and connect with local communities wherever they go.
